Anytone 878 UVII Plus

As a first radio it's been a great choice. It's feature rich (analog, DMR, APRS, GPS) is giving me a lot of flexibility to experiment and figure out what direction(s) I want to go.

If you are far from major repeaters, DMR with a hotspot can let you make worldwide QSOs with an HT.
