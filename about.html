I recently read the Remembrance of Earth's Past (Three Body Problem) trilogy by Liu Cixin. It deeply affected me.

Between that and listening to Daniel and Kelly's Extraordinary Universe podcast, I have found a new passion for exploring the electromagnetic spectrum through amateur radio.

I am beginning with VHF/UHF and DMR on a 5-watt HT with a Technician license. My plan is to go for my Extra license later this year, with aspirations for eventual HF DXing. Currently learning CW.

Most importantly, I am enjoying learning more about electromagnetism every day.
