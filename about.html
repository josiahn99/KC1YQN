about

I came to amateur radio in late 2014 at the convergence of two interests. One was reading Liu Cixin’s Remembrance of Earth’s Past (Three-Body Problem) trilogy. The other was listening to the Daniel and Kelly’s Extraordinary Universe podcast. Together, they sparked a renewed curiosity — about the universe and the electromagnetic field that shapes our understanding of it.

Radio became a hands-on way to explore the electromagnetic spectrum. I currently operate VHF/UHF and DMR with a Technician license, using a 5-watt handheld. I’m learning CW and plan to pursue my Extra license, with the goal of operating HF and working DX.

I enjoy the steady practice of learning— both the physics behind radio and the craft of communication.
